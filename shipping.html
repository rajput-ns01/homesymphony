Shipping and Returns

Shipping

We offer free standard shipping on all orders. Orders are typically processed within 1-2 business days and shipped via standard shipping, which takes 3-5 business days for delivery.

If you need expedited shipping, please contact us for options and pricing.

Returns

If you're not completely satisfied with your purchase, you may return it within 30 days for a full refund or exchange. To be eligible for a return, your item must be unused and in the same condition that you received it. It must also be in the original packaging.

Please contact us to initiate a return and receive a return authorization number. Returns without a valid return authorization number will not be accepted.

Once your return is received and inspected, we will issue a refund to your original payment method within 7-10 business days. Please note that shipping costs are non-refundable, and you will be responsible for return shipping fees unless the return is due to a mistake on our part.
