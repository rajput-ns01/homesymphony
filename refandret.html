Refund and Returns Policy

Refund Policy

We want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase. If you're not happy for any reason, we'll gladly provide a refund or exchange within 30 days of your purchase.

To be eligible for a return, your item must be unused and in the same condition that you received it. It must also be in the original packaging.

Some items are non-returnable, including perishable goods, gift cards, and downloadable software products.

If you need to return an item, simply contact us with your order number and details about the product you would like to return. We'll respond promptly with instructions for how to return items from your order.

Returns

We understand that sometimes things don't work out as planned. If you need to return an item, we're here to help.

To initiate a return, please contact us and provide your order number and details about the product you would like to return. We'll respond promptly with instructions for how to return items from your order.

Please note that shipping costs are non-refundable. If you receive a refund, the cost of return shipping will be deducted from your refund.
